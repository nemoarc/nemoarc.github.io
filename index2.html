    We are an Amateur Radio club in Northeast Missouri. We are in Adair county and have members from the surrounding area.
    The station Call sign is W0CBL
     We operate a 2meter repeater on 145.130-  156.7 tone
    Our club meets on the second Sunday of every month at 1:30pm
     at The Adair County Ambulance Training Center 606 W Potter. 

    However During the pandemic we are meeting virtually using ZOOM

     We are an Amateur Radio club in Northeast Missouri.
    Located in Adair county with members from the surrounding areas.
     The club station Call is W0CBL
     We operate a 2meter repeater on 145.130- 156.7 tone
    We also operate a repeater on 442.525+ 156.7 tone
    and currently an APRS digipeater

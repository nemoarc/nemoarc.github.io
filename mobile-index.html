Northeast Missouri Amateur Radio Club

Serving Adair County and Surrounding Area
   

 Welcome to the NEMOARC Web Page

 We are an Amateur Radio club in Northeast Missouri.
Located in Adair county with members from the surrounding areas.
 The club station Call is W0CBL
 We operate a 2meter repeater on 145.130-

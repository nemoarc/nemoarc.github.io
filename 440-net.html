440 Digital Net info


What is the 440 digital net?

In order for more usage for our 70cm repeater it has been proposed to use it at least some of the time for digital keyboard to keyboard coms.

We will be using the free fldigi in MFSK64 mode for the communications.

What Do I need to participate in the net?

You will need at minimum a 70cm radio that has been setup to use our repeater that is

a frequency of 442.750MHz + offset and a tone of 156.7Hz

You will also need a PC or raspberry pi and an interface to go between the radio and PC

That could be one of  several options

Either a signalink    

Or a digirig

These are the 2 easiest solutions as the supply ready made cables for many radios. 

click on the pictures for a link where to buy

For an option that may take a bit of soldering of cables you could use

A usb sound dongle


and use vox to key the radio. or on a pi one of the gpio pins cand be setup as a ptt circuit

There are also pi hats to do this sort of thing.
If you are lucky enough to have a radio with built in usb port then you have all you need except for a usb cable.

More about the fldigi software


The FLdigi  software will need to be setup with your sound device the following screens show how this is done.
First you will need to open the config dialog


Then once there click the plus next to sound card  and devices to setup your audio in and outs


Another thing to keep in mind is some interface ports on the various radios do not engage the radios squelch.
Fortunately fldigi has that covered
see the arrows pointing to the squelch toggle and slider. set the slider just above green column when radio is not recieve=ing a signal to keep garbage text
from filling the yellow area

Licensing

Get licensed and join this interesting hobby. Communications between

hunting buddies, Emergency situations, talk to people across town or

worldwide. Voice, Morse code, use your computer. Point your

handheld antenna up and use a satellite. It’s up to you.


NEMOARC or Your local amateur radio club can get you going and provide testing.

NEMOARC Club History 





Not many of the early club members are still around to ask but this is what is known of the history of the nemoarc.

Chuck Porter WB0ZHB has told us that the club was founded in the 1940's by his father Charlie Porter W0YRL, Lester Dent W0CBL, Hershal Shelton and Joe Stogsdell.
Lester Dent was a pulp fiction writer who published under the name Doc Savage. Charlie Porter was an engineer and on air personality at the KIRX am radio station. The club met at the Bartlett Radio Shop at the north end of Main street. The owner of the shop Harold Bartlett was licensed after the club formed as WA0DHR. other additional members of the club were Charlie and Don  Sinock from Laplata who were also MARS members.

Anyone having any more info, corrections and  photos please email nemoarc@gmail.com
